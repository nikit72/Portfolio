NIKI TRAN

While navigating the many avenues in college, it was at after exploring many different possibilities that I found and absolutely fell in love with design. I’ve always been drawn to art as a form of self-expression and creativity, but it wasn’t until I dove into designing in college that I found such a strong passion for my work.

Creating with empathy at the forefront for different communities, I learned of design's power to improve and reflect people’s lives and identities.

As a designer, I aspire to create environments that empower and assist my users in their lives by celebrating their stories and experiences. With such power, I design not for myself but for the betterment of others, and I am so excited to create!

Thank you for taking your time to learn about me! I love sharing stories, so feel free to chat with me at niki.tran72@gmail.com.
